HOW IT ALL
STARTED

Incorporated in 2007, Radimax Linen Services (RLS) entered the Malaysian commercial linen industry with a new proposition – a full circle service throughout the supply chain.

Our story and credential is founded on more than two decades of experience managing laundry for the public healthcare sector – the most stringent and demanding segment of the linen industry.
From supply to laundry to logistics, RLS’s linen services is integrated into your daily operation but fully handled by our professional team.

With the hassle of linen management out of your daily schedule, all your time and resources can now be focused on your real priority – running and growing your business to a new height.

More than 25 years of experience in healthcare linen industry.

We accommodate your business as you see fit:

  • Size/type of establishment
  • Sales traffic
  • Existing infrastructure
  • Own or lease
